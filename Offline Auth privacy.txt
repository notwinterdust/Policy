PRIVACY POLICY

App: OTP Authenticator
Last updated: 19 May 2026

OVERVIEW

OTP Authenticator ("the app") is a two-factor authentication app that
generates time-based one-time passwords (TOTP) on your device. The app is
designed to work fully offline. This policy explains how the app handles
your information.

DATA WE COLLECT

We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal or device data.

The app has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking.
It does not connect to any server, and your information never leaves your
device.

DATA STORED ON YOUR DEVICE

The following data is created and stored only on your device:

- Authentication accounts you add (account name, issuer, and the secret key
  used to generate codes). Secret keys are kept in the device's encrypted
  secure storage.
- App settings, such as your chosen language and whether App Lock is enabled.

This data is never sent anywhere. It is removed when you delete an account
within the app or uninstall the app.

BACKUPS

You can choose to export a backup file or QR codes. Backups are created only
when you request them and are saved or shared to the location you choose.
Encrypted backups are protected with a password you set (AES-256). You are
responsible for storing backup files securely. The app does not upload or
have access to backup files after they are created.

PERMISSIONS

- Camera: used only to scan QR codes when adding or importing accounts. No
  images are stored or transmitted.
- Biometrics / device credentials: used only to unlock the app when App Lock
  is enabled. Biometric data is handled by your device's operating system and
  is never accessed by the app.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The app does not collect any data from anyone, including children. No
personal information is gathered regardless of the user's age.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If this policy changes, the updated version will be provided with the app
and the "Last updated" date above will be revised.
